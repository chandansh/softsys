Company Overview

SOFTSYS is an innovative Information Technology company established in 2005 and offers Software Product Development and Software Services. Specialized in the area of web portal development, SOFTSYS easily stands as the leader in Open Source technology space (more specifically Liferay) in India and stands amongst the top few globally in this space. We consult, develop projects, conduct trainings, build high-end portals and provide comprehensive service to companies and clients seeking IT solutions. We delight customers through better, faster and cost effective IT solutions. Some of our clients include Xerox, USOncology, Cisco, ABB, CapGemini, Travelocity, Sun Microsystems, BOSCH, GE Healthcare, Skali, BlueStar, LG and many more. We understand the Business! The last decade has seen businesses growing globally and expanding across geographies to meet frenetic growth targets, and localized silos of production, operations and managements were established. In the last few years and the next decade, organizations would constantly invest in consolidation and centralization of data, information, reports, etc. mPower with its reputed and successful list of corporate and government customers has successfully build large portals and web enabled operations for its customers, consolidating and centralizing silos of data in real time or as required to fulfill and service the needs of 'always mobile and logged in' clients and customers. Besides Banking, Finance, Travel, Retail that have moved on to web/online, Governance (Registration, taxes, etc), Compliance Monitoring (Pollution, Waste Management, etc.), Health monitoring, Education sector are other fields that are fast moving towards web enabled and online methods.
